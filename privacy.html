Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 11, 2026

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

ReplyMate ("we," "our," or "us") is an AI-powered reply assistant designed to help users generate thoughtful responses to messages they receive. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the ReplyMate iOS application (the "App").

By using ReplyMate, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

a. Messages You Input

When you paste or type a message into ReplyMate, that message is sent to our backend servers to generate AI-powered reply suggestions. We process the message solely for the purpose of generating replies and do not permanently store the full content of your messages.

b. Basic Usage Data

We may automatically collect limited, non-personal usage information, such as:

  • Timestamp of requests
  • Number of requests made
  • Error logs (e.g., failed generation attempts)
  • Anonymous performance metrics

This data is used exclusively for service stability monitoring, performance improvement, and abuse prevention.

c. Apple Sign-In Information

If you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive a unique identifier from Apple. We do not receive or store your Apple ID password. Sign-in is handled entirely by Apple's secure authentication system.

d. Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, transaction and receipt data is processed through Apple's App Store. We verify subscription status via Apple's receipt verification service. We do not collect or store your payment card information.

We do not collect: Your name, phone number, address, contacts, messages from your chat apps, login credentials for any third-party service, or any biometric data.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To generate AI replies: Your input message is processed by our backend and sent to a third-party AI service to produce reply suggestions.
  • To operate and improve the service: Basic usage data helps us maintain uptime, diagnose errors, and improve reply quality.
  • To manage subscriptions: We verify purchase status with Apple to grant or restrict access to Pro features.
  • To prevent abuse: Request counts and rate limits help ensure fair usage of the service.

3. Third-Party Services

ReplyMate uses the following third-party services:

a. DeepSeek API

When you request AI-generated replies, your input message is forwarded to DeepSeek's API (operated by DeepSeek) for processing. DeepSeek generates the reply text and returns it to our servers. We do not control how DeepSeek processes data. Please refer to DeepSeek's Privacy Policy for more information.

b. Apple Services

Apple Sign-In and App Store In-App Purchases are handled entirely by Apple. We receive only the minimum data necessary (unique identifier and purchase verification) to provide service functionality.

c. Railway (Cloud Hosting)

Our backend servers are hosted on Railway, a cloud infrastructure provider. Data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted in transit using TLS.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other commercial entities.

We may disclose information if required by law, such as in response to a valid legal process or government request.

5. Data Retention

We retain the minimum amount of data necessary to operate the service:

  • Input messages: Processed temporarily to generate replies and not permanently stored.
  • Usage logs: Retained for up to 90 days for analytics and abuse prevention, then anonymized or deleted.
  • Subscription records: Retained as long as your account is active, plus 12 months for billing verification purposes.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 8).

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) for all API communications
  • Secure token-based authentication for API access
  • Limited server access with strict authentication controls
  • Regular security updates and dependency monitoring

7. Children's Privacy

ReplyMate is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete it promptly. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the App or via email (if provided). The "Effective Date" at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
